About me

My tattoo journey started 3 years ago and has led me here you your beautiful city of Manhattan. You’ll find a lot of color in my portfolio. I love working with color, especially neo traditional and anything pop culture. When it comes to other types of art, color is still my go to. I love painting watercolor with acrylic inks.

If I’m not tattooing you can find me watching tv, playing video games, hanging out with my cat, or looking for cool rocks.

I strive to make my chair as comfortable as possible for my clients, everyone is welcome! let’s create something that you’ll love forever!
